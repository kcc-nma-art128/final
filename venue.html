FAQS

  • When do doors open for a show?

    The doors typically open one hour prior to show time. Please refer our Calendar page or www.axs.com for the most up-to-date information.

  • What is your maximum capacity?

    The El Rey Theater has a maximum capacity of 771.

  • Do you sell food and alcohol?

    Yes. You must be over 21 to drink alcohol. You will be asked for ID.

  • I am a journalist interested in covering a show. Can I get a press pass?

    We do not approve press or photo passes. Please contact the artist directly. We do not have their contact information.

  • Do you allow re-entry?

    No. Once your ticket has been scanned you will not be allowed to leave and re-enter the venue.

  • Is there a dress code?

    Shirts and shoes required!

  • Can I bring my camera/video camera?

    No professional recording devices are allowed. This includes professional cameras (any camera with a detachable lens).

  • Do you have a lost and found?

    If you lost something at the El Rey, please contact us.

  • How can I book my band to play here?

    Please send all concert booking inquiries to concerts@goldenvoice.com.



TICKETING FAQS

  • Does your theatre have seats?

    We are a general admission, standing room only venue.

  • Do you have handicapped seating?

    We do accommodate ADA seating. Please contact us ahead of time for more information.

  • Do you offer VIP/bottle service?

    We do not offer bottle service. Depending on the show, we sometimes have the VIP balcony open. We sell tables in the VIP balcony for $150 before the show on our web store or the night of the show. The tables seat four people and include four drink vouchers.

  • I forgot to print my tickets, can you scan them from my smartphone?

    Yes, but please have them up on your phone prior to entering the venue.
