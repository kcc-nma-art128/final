Welcome

For the most authentic Japanese dining atmosphere in Waikiki, Kobe Steak House, Sushi & Bar provides excellence and precision with delicacies. We promise to serve you a wide array of succulent seafood and premium steaks that are carefully selected for quality, freshness and taste.
