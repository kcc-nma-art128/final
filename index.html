About

We are a certified Paul Mitchell Signature Salon that has progressed to groom many of Hawai’i’s greatest artists, performers, and politicians. Mei Au, the founder and owner, has been cutting and styling hair for over 27 years, and is giving back to her craft by bringing in fresh and talented professionals who share her passion for hairstyling. We believe that if you improve the look of a person, their inner person feels better and can then become more productive and interactive in today's “fast paced world.”
